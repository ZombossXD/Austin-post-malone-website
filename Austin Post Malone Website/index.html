Get To Know About Austin Post Malone

Austin Richard Post Was Born On 4 July, 1995, In Syracuse, New York.He Was raised By His Father, Richard Post, And His Stepmother, Jodie. His Father Had Been A DJ In His Youth And Introduced Austin To Many Different Genres Of Music Including Hip Hop, Country And Rock.When Austin Was 9 Years Old, He And His Family Moved To Grapevine, Texas, after His Father Became The Manager Of Concessions For The Dallas Cowboys.Austin Began To Play The Guitar And Auditioned For The American Band Crown The Empire In 2010, But Was Rejected After His Guitar Strings Broke During The Audition.He Credited His Initial Interest In Learning Guitar To The Video Game Guitar Hero.

Career Beginnings

According To Austin, He Chose "Post Malone" As His Stage Name When He Was 14 or 15.The Name Was Rumored To Be A Reference To The Professional Basketball Player Karl Malone,But Austin Later Explained That "Post" Is His Last Name, And He Used A "Rap Name Generator" To Get "Malone".At 16, Using Audio Editor Audacity, Austin Created His First Mixtape, Young and After Them Riches.He Showed It To Some Of His Classmates At Grapevine High School.He was voted "Most Likely to Become Famous" By His Classmates As A Senior In High Cchool.

What Next

At age 17, Austin Released A Parody Synth-Pop Ballad Song Called "Why Don't You Love Me" Under The Alias Leon DeChino, Intended For A Cchool Project. The song Reappeared Online In 2016. Malone Later Referenced The DeChino Alias on His Song "Psycho".

Moving Out

Austin Later Enrolled In Tarrant County College, But Dropped Out.After Leaving College, Austin Moved To Los Angeles With His Longtime Friend Jason Probst, A Professional Game Streamer.

What Happend After Moving Out

After Moving To Los Angeles, Austin, Probst, And Several Other Producers And Artists Formed The Music Group BLCKVRD And Recorded Music Together. Several Members Of The Group, Including Austin, Moved Into A House In The San Fernando Valley Together. While Living In The San Fernando Valley, Austin Met Record Producers FKi 1st And Sauce Lord Rich, Who Formed The Production Team FKi, As Well As Rex Kudo, Who Produced Several Of Austin's Tracks, Including "White Iverson". Austin Recorded The Song Two Days After Writing It. The Lyrics Of "White Iverson" Allude To Basketball Hall Of Fame Player Allen Iverson. In February 2015, Upon Completion, It Was Uploaded To Austin's SoundCloud Account. On 19 July, 2015, Austin Released A Music Video For "White Iverson". The Single Received Praise From Mac Miller And Wiz Khalifa.

Contract Signing

After Hitting One Million Views Within A Month Of Releasing "White Iverson", Austin Quickly Garnered Attention From Record Labels. In August 2015, He Signed A Recording Contract With Republic Records. Austin Went On To Work With A Number Of Prominent Rappers Including 50 Cent, Young Thug, Kanye West, And Others. In August 2015, He Performed At Kylie Jenner's 18th Birthday Party, Where He Met Kanye West, Who Enjoyed His Music, Leading To Him Collaborating With Austin On His Single "Fade" From His Album The Life Of Pablo (2016). On April 20, 2016, Austin Premiered His New Single, "Go Flex" On Zane Lowe's Beats 1 Show.

First Album Released

On December 9, 2016, Austin Released His Debut Studio Album, Stoney. The Album Reached Number Four On The Billboard 200. It Produced The Top-10 Single, "Congratulations", Which Features Quavo And Reached Number Eight On The Billboard Hot 100, As Well As The 20 Single, "White Iverson" From 2015, Which Reached Number 14 On The Hot 100.
